Welcome to ElectricityData

Your one-stop shop for data on the electricity industry in Great Britain

Datasets.Browse, visualise and download datasets. Contribute and curate your own data.

Data is published in units called “datasets” - a parcel of data, for example temperature readings from various weather stations

Organisations.Find datasets published by organisations. Start your own, with public or private datasets.

Organizations are used to create, manage and publish collections of datasets. Users can have different roles within an Organization, depending on their level of authorisation to create, edit and publish

Groups.Arrange datasets into themes, with fine-grained access control.

You can use Groups to create and manage collections of datasets. This could be to catalogue datasets for a particular project or team, or on a particular theme, or as a very simple way to help people find and search your own published datasets
